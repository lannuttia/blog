About Me:

My Professional History:

I'm a software engineer working remotely out of the greater Fayetteville Arkansas area. I graduated from the University of Arkansas in December of 2016 with my Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineering. Right out of college I worked at L3Harris Technologies in Greenville Texas. While there, I discovered my love of Software. I did everything from DSP algorithm development, to web service development, to FE web development, and eventually to CI/CD pipeline development. After several years of working there, I found a job working a J.B. Hunt Transportation. While at J.B. Hunt, I have been working on Spring Boot and Quarkus applications for the Carrier 360 platform.

My Hobbies:

I love dabbling in Linux and BSD system administration, and ricing my own Linux desktop. I also love playing games like Factorio, Feed the Beast (Minecraft with mods), and the occasional shooter like Doom. Despite my love of gaming, I do not own a Windows or Mac computer. All of my computers and networking equipment runs some form of BSD or Linux.
